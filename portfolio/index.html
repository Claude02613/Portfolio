Liquid Expectation

"Liquid Expectation" is a virtual experience exploring the emotional impact of parental expectations in Chinese families. In a dark void, users encounter two text-based "gateways" that offer glimpses into a child's inner world. These gateways, through raw descriptions, reflect deep emotions: helplessness from being ignored, frustration from lack of autonomy, longing for recognition, and yearning for understanding. A carefully crafted sound map guides users through this emotional labyrinth.

Between Frames

"Between Frames" is an experimental immersive video that explores the interplay of love and death through practical effects and digital manipulation. By combining slow-motion footage of color-changing milk experiments with 3D simulations, the piece creates a meditative space where familiar forms dissolve and transform. The work challenges traditional notions of video immersion by deliberately mixing frame rates and textures, creating moments of both intense focus and ethereal transition. Through practical effects and careful pacing inspired by the Japanese concept of "MA" (negative space), the piece invites viewers to find their own moments of immersion in both motion and stillness.

Shadowless Torii Gate

"Shadowless Torri Gate" is an interactive digital environment that explores the intersection of traditional Japanese architecture and glitch aesthetics. Through a minimalist interpretation of the Torri Gate, transformed by transparent materials and dynamic animations, the piece creates a meditative space where digital imperfection meets natural serenity. Set against a dynamic sky with volumetric clouds and changing sunlight, the glitch-styled gate—inspired by Kanizsa's illusory contours—flickers between presence and absence. The environment combines peaceful ocean sounds with unpredictable architectural animations, reflecting on the concepts of Ma and Wabi-sabi.

Speculative Cyberfeminist Future

An practical video piece that critically examines cyberfeminism through a speculative lens. Created in collaboration with collaborator and friend Lucas, the work explores the intersection of gender, transform, technology, and digital autonomy.
